On a new image, you will start with the first keypoint by default. 

- Click sequentially to place keypoints in order
- To move an existing label, drag and drop
- To mark a keypoint as occluded:
  - Click the click the "occluded" checkbox beneath the keypoints selection or 
  - Press the "o" key (as in oscar)
  - Uncheck the "occluded" box to reverse this, then click to place the keypoint
- Ctrl+Z will undo your last label

For project-specific labeling instructions, please see the Labeling Instructions at the top right.
